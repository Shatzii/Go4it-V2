1 · POSITIONING & GROUND RULES

Go4it Sales & Marketing Script Pack

This pack is for anyone who will message, email, call, or present on behalf of Go4it Academy. Follow these scripts, adjust details to the family, and never go off-script on compliance.

Who We Talk To

  • Parents of serious student-athletes (ages ~13–19).
  • Student-athletes themselves (especially on Instagram).
  • Coaches and club directors (multipliers).

What We Sell First

  • Parent Nights (free, weekly, online).
  • StarPath Assessment™ ($199) – academic + athletic readiness assessment.
Rule #1: Early in the relationship, your only hard CTAs are: “Come to Parent Night” or “Do a StarPath Assessment™”. School enrollment and residencies are offered only after an assessment.

Non-Negotiable “Do Not Say” List

  • Do not promise scholarships. (We can be part of their journey, not guarantee outcomes.)
  • Do not guarantee NCAA eligibility. (We help families understand rules and plan.)
  • Do not give immigration, visa, or legal advice.
  • Do not trash other schools, clubs, or coaches. (We are a layer on top, not a replacement.)
2 · LEAD STAGES & GOALS

Pipeline Stages

2.1 Stages

  1. Cold / New Lead: DM, email, or in-person contact.
    • Goal: RSVP for Parent Night or express interest in StarPath Assessment.
  2. Parent Night Attendee: Has shown up live.
    • Goal: Book StarPath Assessment within 24–48 hours.
  3. StarPath Completed: Report delivered.
    • Goal: Place athlete into:
      • Full-time Go4it Academy,
      • StarPath Online track (keep their school), or
      • 12-week Residency (Vienna or Dallas), if fit.
  4. Ongoing: Retention, upgrades, and referrals.
