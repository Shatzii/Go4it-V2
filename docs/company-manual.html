1 · NORTHSTAR

Why We Exist

1.1 Core Problem We Solve

Most serious student-athletes and families feel:

  • Confused by NCAA and international eligibility rules.
  • Underserved by schools that don’t understand elite training schedules.
  • Uncoached academically (no one teaches them how to be a student-athlete).
  • Unverified athletically (no honest measure of their true level).

We solve this by building a clear, verified pathway from:

“Talented & confused” → “Qualified, ready, and seen.”

1.2 Mission

To build a global academy and operating system that gives student-athletes:

  • Academic clarity – where they stand and what to fix.
  • Athletic development – training, habits, and feedback that actually move the needle.
  • Pathways – realistic routes into NCAA, JUCO, NAIA, or international opportunities.

1.3 Vision (3–5 Years)

  • Go4it Academy recognized as the #1 StarPath provider for U.S. and international student-athletes.
  • Vienna and Dallas residencies running full, recurring 12-week cohorts every year.
  • StarPath used by clubs, academies, and schools as the default NCAA readiness OS.

1.4 Brand Promise

To every family, we promise:

“We will tell you the truth about where your athlete stands academically and athletically — and give you a real plan, not hype.”
2 · IDENTITY

What Go4it Academy Is (and Isn’t)

2.1 We Are

  • A homeschool / online-friendly academy built for serious student-athletes.
  • The creator and operator of the StarPath System.
  • A provider of 12-week residencies in Vienna (soccer) and Dallas (football & basketball).
  • A bridge between U.S. and international pathways.

2.2 We Are NOT

  • Not a traditional brick-and-mortar high school.
  • Not a recruiting service that guarantees scholarships.
  • Not a legal, immigration, or visa advisory firm.
  • Not a club trying to replace existing teams. We layer on top of current environments.

2.3 Truth-in-Marketing Disclaimer

This text MUST appear on all academic/eligibility documents, PDFs, and key pages:

Go4it is a homeschool learning provider with American teachers. Credits and official transcripts are issued via U.S. school-of-record partners until Fall 2026. Athlete OS and GAR Testing are non-academic and do not grant credit. No recruiting guarantees. NCAA amateurism and FIFA/FA rules respected. Families remain responsible for local education registration. We do not provide immigration or legal advice.

Staff rule: if you’re not sure whether to include this, include it.

3 · PRODUCT LADDER

What We Sell (Value Stack)

We focus on a small set of products that stack together and support one Northstar: StarPath.

3.1 Ladder Overview

  1. StarPath Assessment™ – entry point, single purchase.
  2. StarPath System (Ongoing)
    • Full-Time Go4it Academy student.
    • StarPath Online (keep their school, we run the OS).
  3. Residencies (Vienna & Dallas, 12 weeks).
  4. Friday Night Lights (FNL) events.
  5. Parent Nights (Tues/Thurs).
  6. GAR Testing & AthleteAI platform layer.

3.2 Product 1 – StarPath Assessment™ (MVP)

Positioning: This is the first product almost every serious family should buy.

Purpose:

  • Show the family where the athlete stands academically (GPA, credits, NCAA core).
  • Show where they stand athletically (GAR 0–100, strengths & gaps).
  • Assign a star rating (0–5 Star) and level projection (D1/D2/NAIA/JUCO/EU).
  • Give a clear plan for the next 6–12 months.

3.3 Product 2 – StarPath System (Ongoing)

A. Full-Time Go4it Academy Student

Who: Athletes ready to let Go4it handle academics + development together.

Includes:

  • Academic program via U.S. partner school-of-record.
  • Weekly academic coaching / check-ins.
  • StarPath dashboard for student & parent.
  • Sport-specific training programs (soccer, American football, basketball).
  • NCAA tracking (courses & GPA) within the StarPath system.

Reference price (internal): ~$399/month (final pricing on website supersedes).

B. StarPath Online (Keep Their School)

Who: Athletes who stay in their current school but need NCAA & development support.

Includes:

  • NCAA tracking and core course planning.
  • Academic planning and accountability.
  • StarPath dashboard + development recommendations.

Reference range: ~$149–$199/month (to be finalized).

Not every athlete has to change schools. StarPath Online lets us serve them powerfully and profitably while they stay where they are.

3.4 Product 3 – 12-Week Residencies (Vienna & Dallas)

Vienna Residency (Soccer):

  • 12-week high-performance soccer + academics + culture.
  • Target: U.S.-based players coming to Europe.
  • Planned dates: ~Feb 2 – Apr 3, 2026.

Dallas Residency (Football & Basketball):

  • 12-week American football + AAU-style basketball.
  • Target: European/international athletes coming to the U.S.
  • Planned dates: ~July 3 – Sept 25, 2026 (including July 4th weekend).

Working Price: $10,000 USD per 12-week program (excluding flights and personal spending).

Non-negotiable rule: No residency without a StarPath Assessment first.

3.5 Product 4 – Friday Night Lights (FNL)

Friday Night Lights events are a mix of high-energy training, combine-style testing, and community building.

  • Gives local athletes exposure to Go4it culture.
  • Collects leads and baseline data (mini GAR + contact info).
  • Invites top fits to StarPath Assessment & Parent Nights.

FNL is not framed as “make-or-break tryouts.” It’s a learning environment and front door to the ecosystem.

3.6 Product 5 – Parent Nights

Tuesday – Parent Info / Demo Night

  • Educate on NCAA rules, academic traps, international issues.
  • Explain StarPath and Go4it Academy.
  • Primary CTA: StarPath Assessment™.

Thursday – Parent Decision Night

  • Recap, share wins, answer deeper questions.
  • Discuss full options: StarPath System, school, and residencies.
  • Primary CTA: StarPath Assessment + enrollment where appropriate.

3.7 Platform Layer – GAR & AthleteAI

  • GAR Testing: Verified 0–100 Game Athletic Rating using structured drills.
  • AthleteAI Platform: Internal software where:
    • StarPath dashboard lives.
    • Reports and plans are stored.
    • NCAA tracking and tasks can be managed.
4 · STARPATH SYSTEM

StarPath Deep Dive

4.1 Inputs → Processing → Outputs

Inputs

  • Transcripts / grade reports.
  • Current and past course lists.
  • Sport and primary position.
  • Anthropometrics (height, weight) and testing numbers.
  • Athlete goals (NCAA level, pro, Europe, etc.).
  • Behavior / routine info (sleep, practice, study habits).

Processing

  • NCAA core mapping and GPA calculation.
  • Academic Readiness Index (ARI 0–100).
  • GAR Athletic Rating (0–100).
  • Star Rating (0–5 Star) based on ARI + GAR + habits.
  • NCAA level projection (D1/D2/D3/NAIA/JUCO/EU).
  • 12-month action plan (academics, training, behavior).
