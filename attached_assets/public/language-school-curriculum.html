Neurodivergent-Friendly Features

  • Multi-sensory learning approaches: Visual flashcards, audio recordings, physical movement activities
  • Clear structure and expectations: Consistent class format, visual schedules, and advance previews
  • Frequent breaks and activity switching: 15-20 minute activity blocks with 3-5 minute breaks
  • Visual supports: Color-coding for grammatical gender, visual vocabulary maps
  • Alternative assessment options: Choice of written, oral, or project-based assessments

Assessment Methods

  • Weekly Vocabulary Quizzes (20%): 10-15 words or phrases, available in written or audio format
  • Conversation Practice (30%): Structured role-play scenarios assessed for comprehensibility and vocabulary use
  • Cultural Project (20%): Research and present on an aspect of Spanish-speaking culture
  • Final Assessment (30%): Comprehensive assessment covering all modules through a choice of written exam, conversation, or creative project
