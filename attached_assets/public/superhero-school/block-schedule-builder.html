Schedule Tips

Tips for Block Scheduling

  • Color-code similar classes or subjects
  • Schedule most challenging classes when you're most alert
  • Add break times between demanding activities
  • Include transition time between classes
  • Don't forget to schedule homework time

ADHD-Friendly Strategies

  • Balance challenging classes with preferred ones
  • Schedule movement breaks
  • Use the lunch period strategically
  • Print your schedule and keep it visible

Dyslexia-Friendly Organization

  • Include room numbers for easy reference
  • Note teacher names to avoid confusion
  • Include visual cues in your notes for each class
  • Make a checklist of materials needed for each class
