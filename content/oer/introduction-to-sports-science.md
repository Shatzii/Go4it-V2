# Introduction to Sports Science

Overview

This short open-educational resource introduces core concepts in sports science for young athletes and families: training principles, basic anatomy, recovery, and measuring improvement. It's designed as a self-study module that pairs with simple drills and a weekly plan.

Course Outline

1. Training principles: overload, progression, specificity.
2. Basic anatomy & movement patterns.
3. Recovery basics: sleep, nutrition, and load management.
4. Simple testing & tracking to measure progress.

How to use

Download the materials, work through the weekly plan, and optionally submit your GAR snapshot to a coach for feedback.
