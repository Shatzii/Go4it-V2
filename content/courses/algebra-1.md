- Level: Beginner / Middle School
- Resources: Practice problems, worked examples, short quizzes

## Lesson 1 â€” What is a quadratic equation?

A quadratic equation is a second-order polynomial equation in a single variable x with a nonzero coefficient for x^2. The standard form is:

```
ax^2 + bx + c = 0
```

Where a, b and c are constants and a != 0.
