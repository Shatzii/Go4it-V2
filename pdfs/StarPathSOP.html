StarPath Assessment ‚Äì Internal SOP

This SOP ensures every StarPath Assessment is delivered at the same level of quality, accuracy, and clarity, regardless of who on the team completes it. Follow these steps exactly so families receive a consistent experience and we can scale assessments without losing trust.

StarPath Promise: In 48 hours, a family should know exactly where their athlete stands academically, athletically, and behaviorally, and what to do for the next 12 months.
1 ¬∑ REQUIRED INPUTS

1. Documents Needed Before You Start

Do not begin calculating until these are received:

  • Completed StarPath Intake Form.
  • Latest academic transcript(s) and current class schedule.
  • Any standardized test scores (SAT, ACT, GCSE, Abitur, etc.).
  • Athletic measurables (40 yd / 30 m, vertical, shuttle, etc.).
  • Link to highlight film or game footage.
  • Club/school coach name and contact (if provided).

Optional but helpful: NCAA Eligibility Center ID, injury history, previous schools, special education notes.

2 ¬∑ SCORING MODEL

2. StarPath Scoring Model

2.1 Academic Readiness Index (ARI ‚Äì 0‚Äì100)

ARI is our academic backbone. It weighs:

  • GPA / Overall Average ‚Äì 40%
  • NCAA Core Course Progress ‚Äì 25% (out of 16 core courses).
  • English Progress ‚Äì 15% (aiming for 4 years / equivalents).
  • Math Progress ‚Äì 10% (aiming for 3 years / equivalents).
  • Consistency ‚Äì 10% (trend up/down across years).

ARI = 0.40√óGPA component + 0.25√óCore Progress + 0.15√óEnglish + 0.10√óMath + 0.10√óConsistency

Color bands: 90‚Äì100 = Green (College ready) ¬∑ 75‚Äì89 = Yellow (On path) ¬∑ <75 = Red (At risk).

2.2 General Athletic Readiness (GAR ‚Äì 0‚Äì100)

  • Speed ‚Äì 30%
  • Power / Explosiveness ‚Äì 20%
  • Agility / Change of Direction ‚Äì 20%
  • Technical Skill ‚Äì 20%
  • Position IQ / Game Feel ‚Äì 10%

Map GAR ‚Üí Stars: 1‚òÖ (<60) ¬∑ 2‚òÖ (60‚Äì69) ¬∑ 3‚òÖ (70‚Äì77) ¬∑ 4‚òÖ (78‚Äì87) ¬∑ 5‚òÖ (88‚Äì100).

2.3 Behavioral Readiness Index (BRI ‚Äì 0‚Äì100)

Use intake form, communication, coach notes:

  • Attendance & reliability.
  • Coachability & response to feedback.
  • Goal clarity & self-awareness.
  • Time management & follow-through.

2.4 Combined StarPath Score

StarPath Score = 0.40√óARI + 0.35√óGAR + 0.25√óBRI

This combined score guides our pathway recommendations (D1/D2/JUCO/NAIA, StarPath Online vs Full-Time vs Residency).

3 ¬∑ 8-STEP WORKFLOW

3. Standard Workflow for Every Assessment

Step 1 ‚Äì Intake Validation (5‚Äì10 minutes)

  • Check that all sections of the intake form are complete.
  • Verify transcript is readable and dates/grades make sense.
  • Confirm sport, primary position, grad year, and country.
  • Log athlete into StarPath dashboard with status ‚ÄúINTAKE RECEIVED‚Äù.

Step 2 ‚Äì Academic Processing (10‚Äì15 minutes)

  • Convert grades to NCAA 4.0 scale (or nearest equivalent).
  • Count core courses completed vs. needed (out of 16).
  • Tag missing or weak English, Math, Science, Social Studies.
  • Score ARI using the formula; note color band (Green / Yellow / Red).

Step 3 ‚Äì Athletic Analysis (10‚Äì15 minutes)

  • Record measurables: height, weight, 40/30m, vert, shuttle, etc.
  • Watch at least 3‚Äì5 minutes of highlight film (or multiple clips).
  • Score speed, power, agility, skill, and IQ on a 0‚Äì100 rubric.
  • Calculate GAR and star level; identify primary & secondary position fits.

Step 4 ‚Äì Behavior & Habits (5‚Äì10 minutes)

  • Review intake answers on commitment, routines, and goals.
  • Consider communication quality & speed with family.
  • Score BRI (0‚Äì100) with a short justification note.

Step 5 ‚Äì StarPath Score & NCAA Flagging (5 minutes)

  • Calculate combined StarPath Score.
  • Set NCAA status:
    • Green: on pace with core courses and GPA.
    • Yellow: course gaps or borderline GPA.
    • Red: serious deficits or missing key years.
  • Log notes inside internal dashboard (what you‚Äôre seeing / concerns).

Step 6 ‚Äì StarPath Card (Front)

  • Generate card using template (name, grad year, city, sport/position).
  • Include GAR, star rating, key measurable, and position fit.
  • Add tags like ‚ÄúNext Up Prospect‚Äù, ‚ÄúElite Speed‚Äù, ‚ÄúDevelopmental‚Äù.

Step 7 ‚Äì StarPath Report (Back)

The detailed report MUST include:

  • Transcript summary and ARI explanation.
  • NCAA eligibility status & red/yellow flags.
  • Athletic scouting notes and position fit.
  • Behavioral snapshot and coachability notes.
  • 12-week academic plan and 12-week training plan.
  • Recruiting roadmap (what to do in the next 12 months).

Step 8 ‚Äì Delivery & Offer

  • Email PDF card + full report to family.
  • Offer 20‚Äì30 minute review call.
  • On the call, present:
    • StarPath Subscription (online or full-time) as next step.
    • Residency invitation if appropriate (Vienna / Dallas).
