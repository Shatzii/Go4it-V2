Understanding GPA

GPA Calculation Method

Your GPA is calculated by dividing the total number of grade points earned by the total number of credit hours attempted.

Weighted vs. Standard GPA

Weighted GPA gives additional points for Honors courses (0.5 extra) and AP courses (1.0 extra). Standard GPA treats all courses equally.

Grade Points

  • A+ / A: 4.0 points
  • A-: 3.7 points
  • B+: 3.3 points
  • B: 3.0 points
  • B-: 2.7 points
  • C+: 2.3 points
  • C: 2.0 points
  • C-: 1.7 points
  • D+: 1.3 points
  • D: 1.0 points
  • D-: 0.7 points
