Complete StarPath Pathway

From early foundations to post-graduate professional prep — a single, coherent academic + athletic + global pathway.

Phase Map · Age 6 through 22
Elementary · Middle · High School · Post-Grad
Phase 0 · Elementary Foundations (6–10)

Early Beginners Foundation™ accelerators:

  • Literacy and numeracy fundamentals.
  • Play-based global awareness and basic German/Spanish.
  • Movement skills and positive relationship with sport.
Phase 1 · Middle School · StarPath Pioneer (11–14)
  • Advanced reading, writing, math, and science.
  • Intro to coding, digital portfolio, global issues.
  • Athletic foundations and readiness for high school acceleration.
Phase 2 · High School Acceleration (14–18)
  • Two 12-week residencies (9th & 11th) or online equivalents.
  • German + global studies + digital media + leadership.
  • NCAA-informed core courses and documented academics.
Phase 3 · Global Bridge (17–19)
  • Senior-year alternative featuring college-level work.
  • Language certification, global relations, digital business.
  • Enhanced university and scholarship positioning.
Phase 4 · Post-Graduate AA (18–22)
  • Associate degree combined with professional internships.
  • Sports management, analytics, media, and governance.
  • Direct career pathways or transfer into 4-year degrees.
Neurodivergent & Custom Pathways
  • Adapted curriculum and pacing via Neurodivergent Converter.
  • Multiple modes of evidence: video, audio, visual, written.
  • Executive function scaffolds built into the HDR workflow.
Admissions & Readiness

Clear steps for parents, athletes, clubs, and schools to plug into StarPath Residency, Day, or Online programs.

Who Thrives in StarPath
Fit · Expectations · Commitments
Ideal Student Profile
  • Curious, coachable, and open to new cultures.
  • Serious about school and sport in equal measure.
  • Ready to live like a college student with support.
Family Commitments
  • Alignment with homeschool / school-of-record partnership.
  • Willingness to support travel, transitions, and logistics.
  • Understanding of NCAA amateurism and eligibility rules.
Club & School Partners
  • Desire to create long-term pathways, not one-off tours.
  • Openness to aligning training with documentation systems.
  • Vision for global opportunities for their top players.
Application Steps · Parents & Athletes
Vienna Residency · Day · Online
Step 1 · Inquiry
  • Submit online interest form (Residency, Day, or Online).
  • Share current school, sport, and graduation timeline.
  • Clubs/schools can inquire for group placements.
Step 2 · Blueprint Call
  • 30–45 minute call with Go4it / StarPath advisor.
  • Clarify academic status, goals, and eligibility.
  • Confirm best track: Residency, Day, or Online.
Step 3 · Documentation & Placement
  • Upload transcripts and coach references.
  • Review tuition, housing, and travel details.
  • Confirm start date and onboarding timeline.

Every student receives a personalized pathway plan before departure — aligning school-of-record, NCAA considerations, and StarPath modules.
