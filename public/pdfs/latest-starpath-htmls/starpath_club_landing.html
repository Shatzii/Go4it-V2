Development & Documentation

The StarPath Human Development Record™ turns your daily work with players into a long-term development asset — for you, families, and recruiters.

Six-Pillar Human Development Record™
Holistic · Measurable · NCAA-Informed
Physical & Performance
  • Testing, GPS (where available), HRV, movement screen results.
  • Training load and readiness tracking.
  • Injury history and return-to-play documentation.
Technical & Tactical
  • Mechanics timelines by position (QB, striker, guard, etc.).
  • Video clips with coach and player annotations.
  • Decision-making and game intelligence notes.
Mental & Emotional
  • Mindset journals and reflection prompts.
  • Stress and confidence tracking over the season.
  • Leadership behaviors documented with real examples.
Nutrition & Recovery
  • Sleep, hydration, and recovery habits.
  • Meal documentation during camps or residencies.
  • Education modules on fueling for performance.
Academic & Language
  • Coursework tied to sport (analytics, media, leadership).
  • German/English language immersion documentation.
  • Credit mapping via U.S. school-of-record partners.
Global Scholar-Athlete Portfolio
  • Recruiting-ready profile with video, stats, and academics.
  • Cultural immersion, language progress, leadership evidence.
  • Narrative that explains who the athlete is — not just what they did.

You control what data you want, how often you receive reports, and who they’re shared with.
