Go4it Academy · Who We Are & How It Works

1 · Who We Are
Mission · Vision · What Go4it Is / Is Not

Go4it Sports Academy is a global training and education system for serious student-athletes. We connect local fields to college and pro opportunities through a verified StarPath Academy™ pathway that blends academics, athletic development, and character growth.

Our Mission

To provide a verified academic pathway and elite athletic development for student-athletes who want NCAA or professional opportunities without sacrificing education, character, or eligibility.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted international pathway from local fields to college and professional locker rooms — with Denver & Vienna as dual hubs connecting the U.S. and Europe.

What Go4it Academy Is

  • A homeschool learning provider with American teachers and U.S. school-of-record partners.
  • An NCAA-informed pathway with transcript audits, core course planning, and eligibility guidance.
  • A high-performance training environment for soccer, American football, and select sports.
  • A data-driven development system (StarPath) that tracks progress across academics, athletics, and behavior.

What Go4it Academy is Not

  • We do not guarantee scholarships, contracts, or specific recruiting outcomes.
  • We do not replace immigration, visa, or legal counsel.
  • We do not promise admission to any specific college or professional club.

StarPath Assessment™ = Transcript + GAR Testing + behavior ARI (Academic Rigor Index) = NCAA progress & core rigor GAR (Game Athletic Rating) = Performance data from testing & video Behavior / Leadership Score = Habits, effort, and culture fit Star Rating (0–5★) = Combined profile that updates as students grow

2 · Complete StarPath Educational Journey
Ages 6–22 · One Coherent System

StarPath Academy™ is a complete, aligned pathway from early elementary through post-graduate study. Families can enter at any phase and still remain on track for graduation and NCAA eligibility.

Phase 0 Early Beginners Foundation™ (Ages 6–10)

  • Colorado Standards-aligned literacy and numeracy foundations.
  • Movement literacy and playful athletic development.
  • Early executive function: routines, checklists, planning.
  • Global awareness and safe digital citizenship.
  • Mode: In-Person (Denver/Vienna), Online, or Hybrid.

Phase 1 StarPath Pioneer Program™ (Ages 11–14)

  • Advanced middle school academics (ELA, Math, Science, Social Studies).
  • World languages (German/Spanish), global studies, and STEM integration.
  • Digital portfolio launch and early high school credit opportunities.
  • Aligned to Colorado Academic Standards and NCAA-ready rigor.

Phase 2 High School Accelerator (Ages 14–18)

  • Full high school academics (online, hybrid, or partner schools).
  • Two 12-week European residencies delivering 3.0 NCAA credits each.
  • GAR™ Testing, film analysis, and AthleteAI integration.
  • Transcript audits and NCAA core course alignment.

Phase 3 Global Bridge Program™ (Ages 17–19)

  • ≈12 college-level credits in International Relations, German B1/B2, Digital Business, and Leadership.
  • Senior-year alternative with European professional exposure.
  • Designed to enhance university applications and gap-year value.

Phase 4 Post-Graduate AA Program™ (Ages 18–22)

  • 60-credit Associate of Arts degree (sports management, business, media specializations).
  • Internships with European clubs and organizations.
  • Direct pathways into university or sport/business careers.

Neurodivergent NeuroFlex™ & ND Specialization

  • Supports for ADHD/ADD, ASD, dyslexia, processing challenges, and 2E students.
  • Multi-format content (text, audio, video, diagrams) and multi-path assessments.
  • Executive function coaching, sensory-aware schedules, and movement integration.

5 · NCAA, Colorado Standards & Compliance
Protecting Options · No False Promises

NCAA Core Concepts

  • Core Courses: NCAA-approved English, math, science, social studies, and world languages.
  • Core GPA: GPA calculated only from approved core courses.
  • Amateurism: No pay-for-play or professional contracts while maintaining eligibility.
  • Sliding Scale: Balance between GPA and test scores (when required) for Division I/II.

How Go4it Supports NCAA Readiness

  • Transcript audits and StarPath reports that highlight NCAA core status.
  • Course sequencing recommendations aligned with NCAA guidelines.
  • Partnership with U.S. school-of-records for official credit and transcripts (until Fall 2026).
  • Documentation of hours, assessments, and rigor for all residency and online courses.

Colorado Academic Alignment

Our syllabus system is explicitly mapped to Colorado Academic Standards (CAS) across ELA, Math, Science, Social Studies, World Languages, Computer Science, and PWR (Postsecondary and Workforce Readiness). This provides a stable anchor for families who may transition back to traditional schools.

We protect options. We do not promise outcomes. That means: verified academics, clear records, and honest guidance.

7 · Parent FAQ
Most Common Questions Answered
Is Go4it a “real school” or just training?

Go4it is a homeschool learning provider with American teachers. We partner with accredited U.S. schools-of-record to issue credits and official transcripts until Fall 2026. Our curriculum is college-prep, NCAA-informed, and aligned with Colorado standards.

Can my child return to a traditional school later?

Yes. Because our courses are aligned with Colorado standards and backed by U.S. school-of-record partners, students can typically transition back into traditional schools. Final placement always depends on the receiving school’s policies, but we design for portability and clarity.

Will this help with college recruiting?

We cannot guarantee scholarships or offers. What we do provide is: verified academic records, NCAA core alignment, high-level film and testing, international experience, and a professional environment that college coaches recognize and respect.

How do you support neurodivergent students?

Through our NeuroDivergent Curriculum Converter™, NeuroFlex™ system, and ND Specialization track, we adjust how content is delivered and demonstrated (multi-format, multi-path assessments, executive function coaching) without lowering expectations or rigor.

Can we do this fully online if we can’t travel?

Yes. The Silver Steady™ pathway allows families to stay in their home country while completing the entire StarPath journey online, from early elementary to post-graduate AA — with NCAA-informed design and Colorado academic alignment.

How do we start?

Step 1: Schedule a StarPath consultation. Step 2: Submit transcripts and complete the intake form. Step 3: Receive a personalized education + sport roadmap for your child. Step 4: Enroll in the appropriate phase (Early Beginners, Pioneer, Accelerator, Bridge, AA).
