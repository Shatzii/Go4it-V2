Built for Neurodivergent Minds

Our platform is specifically designed to support students with dyslexia, ADHD, autism spectrum, and other learning differences through comprehensive accessibility features.

Dyslexia Support

Specialized fonts, adjustable letter spacing, reading rulers, and color overlays make reading accessible and enjoyable for students with dyslexia.

ADHD Accommodation

Focus mode, distraction reduction, break timers, and chunked content help students with ADHD maintain attention and manage energy effectively.

Autism-Friendly Design

Consistent layouts, predictable navigation, reduced animations, and sensory-aware design create a comfortable learning environment for autistic students.

Personalized Settings

Each student can customize their learning interface to match their specific needs, with settings that persist across sessions and devices.

Our Approach to Neurodivergent Learning

Strength-Based Learning

We focus on identifying and building upon each student's unique strengths rather than remediating perceived deficits. This approach builds confidence and genuine mastery while honoring neurodivergent thinking styles.

Small Class Sizes

With a maximum of 15 students per class and dedicated teaching assistants, we provide the personalized attention and support that neurodivergent learners need to thrive in a collaborative environment.

Gamified Learning

Our curriculum incorporates game-based learning approaches that leverage natural motivation, provide immediate feedback, and create engaging pathways for mastering academic content.
