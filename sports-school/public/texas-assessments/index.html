‚ôø Testing Accommodations for Neurodivergent Learners

ADHD Support
Extended time, frequent breaks, reduced distractions, fidget tools, and alternative seating arrangements.
Dyslexia Accommodations
Text-to-speech technology, larger fonts, dyslexia-friendly colors, and oral response options.
Autism Spectrum Support
Structured environment, visual schedules, noise-reducing headphones, and predictable routines.
Executive Function Support
Organizational aids, step-by-step instructions, progress monitoring, and task breakdown strategies.
Technology Accommodations
Speech-to-text software, word processing tools, calculators, and assistive technology devices.
Environmental Modifications
Separate testing room, adjusted lighting, temperature control, and minimal visual distractions.
