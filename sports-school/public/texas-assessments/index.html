🎯 STAAR Testing Requirements by Grade

Reading Language Arts
Grades 3-8

Comprehensive reading comprehension, vocabulary, and written expression aligned with Texas Essential Knowledge and Skills.

Mathematics
Grades 3-12

Mathematical reasoning, problem-solving, and computational skills progressing through algebraic concepts.

Science
Grades 5, 8, & EOC

Scientific inquiry, laboratory skills, and understanding of physical, life, and earth sciences.

Social Studies
Grade 8 & EOC

Historical analysis, geographic skills, citizenship, and understanding of government and economics.

End-of-Course Exams
High School

Algebra I, Biology, English I & II, and U.S. History assessments for graduation requirements.

STAAR Alt 2
Special Education

Alternative assessment for students with significant cognitive disabilities, based on alternate achievement standards.

♿ Testing Accommodations for Neurodivergent Learners

ADHD Support
Extended time, frequent breaks, reduced distractions, fidget tools, and alternative seating arrangements.
Dyslexia Accommodations
Text-to-speech technology, larger fonts, dyslexia-friendly colors, and oral response options.
Autism Spectrum Support
Structured environment, visual schedules, noise-reducing headphones, and predictable routines.
Executive Function Support
Organizational aids, step-by-step instructions, progress monitoring, and task breakdown strategies.
Technology Accommodations
Speech-to-text software, word processing tools, calculators, and assistive technology devices.
Environmental Modifications
Separate testing room, adjusted lighting, temperature control, and minimal visual distractions.
