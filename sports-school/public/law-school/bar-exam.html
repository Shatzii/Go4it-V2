  • Public Joint Stock Company (PJSC)
  • Limited Partnership by Shares
  • Benefit Corporation

  • Foreign ownership is limited to 49% in all business sectors
  • 100% foreign ownership is permitted across all sectors without restriction
  • 100% foreign ownership is permitted in most sectors, except those with strategic impact
  • Foreign companies can only operate through free zones and cannot establish on the mainland
3. Under UAE bankruptcy law, which of the following procedures is designed to assist a debtor facing financial difficulties to reach a settlement with creditors while continuing to operate the business?
  • Preventive Composition
  • Bankruptcy Declaration
  • Restructuring Liquidation
  • Financial Dissolution
